The Cali Fair, also called Sugarcane Fair, is a festivity held annually with this name since 1958 in the Valle del Cauca city of Cali in Colombia that takes place between December 25 and 30. Its most important events are the Salsódromo and the so-called Superconcert. During its events, local, national and international orchestras participate, representing all danceable tropical rhythms. It is organized by the Corporation of Events, Fairs and Shows of Cali, Corfecali.
